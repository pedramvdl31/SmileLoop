Frequently Asked Questions

What types of photos work best?

SmileLoop works best with clear, front-facing portrait photos. JPEG and PNG formats are supported. The face should be well-lit and not too small in the frame. Group photos work too ‚Äî each visible face may animate.

Is my photo kept private?

Yes. Your photo is processed securely and deleted from our servers after your video is delivered. We never share, sell, or use your photos for any other purpose.

How long does it take?

Most photos are processed in about 30 seconds. Vintage portraits that need colorization may take up to 60 seconds since the AI restores color before animating.

Do I need an account or subscription?

No. SmileLoop is pay-per-use. Upload a photo, see a free preview, and only pay if you want the full high-quality download. No accounts, no recurring charges.

Can I animate old black and white photos?

Yes! Our vintage portraits page uses a special pipeline that colorizes black & white photos before animating them. The AI adds realistic color tones and then brings the face to life.

What is the output format?

SmileLoop generates a short MP4 video (typically 5 seconds) at high resolution. The video loops smoothly and can be shared on social media, sent via message, or saved to your device.
